Global:
- Gym members must not see or access admin-only management pages.

US01:
- Gym members can view a list of available gyms.

US02:
- Gym members can open gym details with location, address, name, and rating.

US03:
- Gym members can create workouts and select exercises for each workout.

US04:
- Gym members can view exercises with muscle group, difficulty, and equipment information.

US05:
- Gym members can view workout duration and added exercises.

US06:
- Gym admins can create, edit, and manage exercises.
- Exercise management pages are restricted to Gym admins.

US07:
- Gym admins can create, edit, and manage gyms.
- Gym management pages are restricted to Gym admins.
